Slow-Cooker Cherry Grunt

2 1/4 pounds frozen sweet cherries
2 cups (10 ounces) all-purpose flour
1 cup (7 ounces) plus 3 tablespoons sugar
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 1/4 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon almond extract
1 tablespoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup plus 2 tablespoons milk
4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted and cooled

Combine cherries ¼ cup flour, 1 cup sugar, lemon juice, 1  teaspoon cinnamon, and almond extract in bowl. Microwave, uncovered, until cherries begin to release their liquid, about 5 minutes, stirring once halfway, through cooking. Stir well, transfer mixture to slow cooker, and spread into even layer.

In large bowl, combine remaining 1 ¾ cups flour, 2 tablespoons sugar, baking powder and salt. Stir in milk and butter until combined. In small bowl, combine remain 1 tablespoon sugar remaining ¼ teaspoon cinnamon. 

Using a greased ¼-cup measure, drop 8 dumplings around perimeter of the slow cooker on top of cherries, leaving center empty. Sprinkle dumplings with cinnamon-sugar mixture. Cover and cook on low until toothpick inserted I center of dumplings comes out clean, about 4 hours.
