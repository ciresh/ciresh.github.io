Cranberry Friut Nut Bread

1 cup (115g) Fresh or frozen cranberries, coarsely chopped
1/2 cup (75g) chopped walnuts
1 tablespoon grated orange peel
2 cups (250g) all purpose flour
1 cup (200g) sugar
1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 tablespoon shortening
3/4 cup orange juice
1 egg, well beaten

Preheat oven to 350F. Generously grease and flour a 9x5 3 loaf pan. Prepare cranberries, nuts and orange peel. 

Set aside. In a bowl mix together flour, sugar, baking powder, salt and soda. Cut in the shortening. Stir in orange juice, egg, and orange peel mixing to just mositen. Fold in the cranberries and nuts. Spoon mixture into prepared pan. Bake 60 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool on reack 15 minutes. Remove from pan, cool completely. Wrap and store overnight.
