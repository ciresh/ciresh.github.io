Macaroni Salad
Source: Cooks Grill and Barbeque Bible

Salt
1 pound elbow macaroni
1 medium celery rib minced
1/4 small red onion
3 hard cooked eggs peeled and diced small
1/4 cup diced sweet pickles
1/4 cup minced fresh parsley leaves
1/4 cup juice from 2 lemons
1 cup mayonnaise
2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
Ground black pepper

Bring 4 quarts of water to a boil in a large pot and add 2 tablespoons salt. Sir in the macaroni and cook until thoroughly done, 10 to 12 minutes, Drain the macaroni into a colander and rinse with cold water until cook. Shake the macaroni dry in the colander and then spread it in an even layer on a rimmed baking sheet lined with paper towels. Let the macaroni dry for 3 minutes. 

Roll the macaroni in paper towels to blot  any remain g moisture and transfer the drained macaroni to a large bowl. Toss with the remaining ingredients and season liberally with salt and pepper to taste. Refrigerate the macaroni salad for at least 1 hour or up to 1 day.
