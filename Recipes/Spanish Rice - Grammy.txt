Spanish Rice
source: Anne Duross (Grammy)

1 cup chopped onion
1 cup chopped celery
1 can tomatoes
1 tsp salt
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1/2 cup cut up cheese
2 cups cooked rice
Ham

Preheat over to 300 degrees.

Cook onions and celery in hot fat until golden bown. Add tomatoes and salt. Simmer 1/2 hr.

Add cut up ham along with brown sugar and cheese. Stir until melted mix with rice and bake in greased and covered casseroles until hot.
