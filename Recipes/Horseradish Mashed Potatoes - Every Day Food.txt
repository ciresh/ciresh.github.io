Horseradish Mashed Potatoes
Every day food
Serves 4; Prep time: 15 minutes; Total time: 35 minutes
	
2 pounds new potatoes, scrubbed and quartered
 Salt
3/4 cup milk
4 tablespoons butter, cut into small pieces
2 tablespoons prepared horseradish
 
n a 5-quart saucepan, cover potatoes with salted water. Bring to a boil; reduce to a simmer. Cook until tender when pierced with the tip of a paring knife, 20 to 25 minutes. Drain; return to pan. Cook over medium-high, stirring, until dry, 1 to 2 minutes.

In a small saucepan, bring milk to a simmer. Mash potatoes with butter, horseradish, hot milk, and 1 teaspoon salt.
If too thin, cook over medium heat, stirring, until thickened.
