Spinach Recipe - Easy Creamed Spinach
From Diana Rattray,
Your Guide to Southern Food.
An easy creamed spinach recipe.
INGREDIENTS:

    * 1/4 cup butter
    * 1/4 cup flour
    * 1 cup milk
    * 1 bag or 2 boxes (16 to 24 ounces) frozen spinach
    * salt and pepper
    * dash nutmeg

PREPARATION:
Melt butter in top of double boiler over low direct heat. Blend in flour until smooth. Cook until mixture is smooth and bubbly. Gradually add milk and cook, stirring, until thickened. Add spinach. Cover and cook over boiling water, stirring occasionally, 15 minutes.
Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
Serves 4.
