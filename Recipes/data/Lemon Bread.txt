Glazed Lemon Bread

1 cup granulated sugar
1/2 cup vegetable shortening
2 eggs (room temperature)
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 cup liquid milk
2 lemons, grated rind
1 teaspoon lemon extract

GLAZE
Juice of 1 lemon
1/2 cup granulated sugar

BREAD
Cream shortening, sugar, grated rind and lemon extract. Add eggs, singly, unbeaten. Beat well. Blend flour and baking powder. Add alternately with milk, about 1/3 each, to mixture, stirring vigorously. Pour into 5 X 9 inch non-stick loaf pan, or line with foil. Bake in 350 degree oven for about 55 minutes or until cake tester comes out dry.

GLAZE
Add lemon juice to granulated sugar. Stir until dissolved. Dribble over top of cake bread when removed from oven.
Cool at least 5 hours before cutting. Keeps well in refrigerator.
