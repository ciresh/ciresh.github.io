All Purpose Cornbread
   
1 1/2 cups flour (7 1/2 ounces)
1 cup (5 1/2 ounces) cornmeal
2 teaspoons baking powder
3/4 teaspoons salt
1/4 teaspoons baking soda
    Combine.   
1 1/2 cup Buttermilk
3/4 cup frozen corn kernals, thawed
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
    Process in food processor until combined, 5 seconds.
2 large eggs
    Add and process until combined, 5 seconds. Gently fold into dry ingredents. 
6 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted and cooled slightly
    Fold into batter until barely combined. Pour into 8x8 or 10" cast iron pan and bake at 400 degrees for 25-35 minutes.
    
Note: This is a modification of orginal recipe. The orginal had 8 tablespoons butter and 1 cup buttermilk.
