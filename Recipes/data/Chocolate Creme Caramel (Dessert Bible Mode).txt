Chocolate Creme Caramel

For the Caramel
1/3 cup water
1 cup granulated sugar
1 1/2 tablespoon light corn syrup

For the Custard

1/2 cup heavy Cream
1 1/2  milk
1/4 cup granulated sugar
2 whole eggs
2 egg yolks
5.5 ounces 70% chocolate
Pinch of salt
3/4 vanilla

Set oven to 350 and place 6 6oz ramakins on sheet try in oven to warm them

Make caramel, pour in cups

Heat milk and cream in heavy saucepan

Mix eggs, sugar and salt in a separate bowl.

When milk mixture is steaming, add 1/2 cup to egg mixture to temper. Repeat until combined.

Cover with foil. Prick the foil to allow steam to escape. Place in hot water bath and cook for 40-60 minutes. Custard should be set and knife should come out clean.

Serve at room tempature.
