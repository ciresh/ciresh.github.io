Mrs. Gartner's Banana Bread1/2 Cup Magarine1 1/2 Sugar2 Eggs2 Cups Flour1/4 Tsp Baking Powder1 Cup mashed bananas1/4 Sour Cream1 tsp Vanilla3/4 tsp baking sodaMix soft magarine, sugar and eggs. Add bananas and cream alternately with flour and rest of ingredients. Bake 35-40 minutes (perhaps 1 hr) in a 9x5x3 loaf pan
