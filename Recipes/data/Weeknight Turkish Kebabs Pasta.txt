Weeknight Turkish Kebabs Pasta

Chicken:
600g/ 1.3 lb Chicken breast
1 tsp salt
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp oregano
1 tsp garlic granules
1/2 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp black pepper
2 tbsp olive oil

Pasta and sauce:
250g pasta cooked to al dente with 1 chicken stock cube
1 medium onion (finely diced)
15g butter
1 red pepper (~100g, finely diced)
2 padron peppers (thinly sliced) (sub shishito)
4 garlic cloves (minced)
30g pepper paste (sub tomato paste)
15g tomato paste
3 tsp oregano
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp sumac
1/2-2 tsp pul biber (Aleppo, sub 1/2 red pepper flakes and paprika)
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp salt (will vary depending on how salty pasta water is)
300-400 ml pasta water
45g cream cheese

Cut chicken into bite size pieces. Combine with spices

Cook onions in butter until translucent. Pull to one side and add chicken. Add peppers, then garlic. Cooks for a few minutes.

Add remaining spices and cooks and thick. Add a bit of the pasta water to degraze, then add cream cheese. When cream cheese is disolved, add pasta.
