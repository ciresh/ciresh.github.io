Chocolate Wafer Ingredients
2 Cups butter, softened
2 1/2 cups granulated sugar
3 large eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
5 cups all purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 1/4 cups unsweented cocoa


Peanut Butter Filling Ingredients
1/3 cup peanut butter
3 tablespoons butter, softened
3 cups confectioner's sugar
1/4 cup milk
1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions
In a large bowl, beat butter, sugar, eggs and vanilla until light and fluffy. Combine flour, baking soda, and cocoa powder in a medium bowl. Gradually stir flour mixture ino sugar mixture until evenly mixed. Divide dough into four equal pieces. Shape each into an 8-10 inch roll. Wrap each roll in waxed paper or plastic wrap. Refridgerate for 2 hours, until firm.

Preheat oven to 350F

Slice chilled dough into 1/4 inch thick slices. Arrange slices on greased baking sheets about 1/2 inch apart. Bake 8 to 10 minutes, until edges are set and cookies are slightly firm on top. Cool on racks.

In a large mixing bowl, cream peanut butter and butter together. Add sugar, milk and vanilla. Mix until smooth.

Assembly

Place 1 teaspoon filling in center of cookie wafer. Press second cookie on top to make sandwich. Store in air tight container for up to one week.

Makes 5 to 6 dozen chocolate sandwich cookies.

From Recipes for Good Cheer, Longaberger
