Peanut Butter Frosting

16 tablespoons unsalted butter
1 cup peanut butter
2 1/4 tablespoons heavy cream
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla
1 1/2 cups confectioners sugar

Glaze

4 oz bittersweet chocolate
4 tablespoons unsalted butter
2 tablespoons light corn syurp

Beat butter,  peanut butter, cream, vanilla and salt on medium-high speed until smooth, about 1 minutes. Reduce speed to medium-low, slowly add sugar and beat until incorporated and smooth, about 3 minutes. Increase speed to medium-high and beat until frosting is light and fluffy, about 5 minutes.

Microwave chocolate, butter and corn syrup in a bowl at 50 percent power until melted and smooth, about 1 minutes. stirring with a rubber spatula half-way through microwaving. Let cool until slightly thickened, about 5 minutes. Pour 1/4 cup glaze in center of caked and spread evenly over top with offset spatula. Drizzle 1/4 cup glaze along top edge of cake, allowing it to drop down sides. Pour remaining glaze over top of cake and smooth into even layer with offset spatula.
