Ricotta Pie

6 Eggs
1 teaspoon lemon extract
1 lb. ricotta cheese
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup milk
1 1/2 tablespoons flour
1/4 teaspoon baking powder

Set oven to 350 degrees.

In a large bowl, beat the eggs and add lemon extract. Add other ingredients one at a time. Mix well. Fill pie shell.

Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes to about and hour. The pie is done when a knife is inserted in the center and comesout clean. Let cool. Refridgerate. Serve cold.

Unbaked pie crust

3 1/2 cups flour
3 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 cup sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup shortening
3 eggs slightly beaten
1/2 cup milk
1 teaspon vanilla


In a medium bowl mix all dry ingredients. In another bowl, mix the shortening and all the liquid ingredients. Add that mixture to the dry mixture. Knead until dough holds its shape. Let rest for 1/2 hour.

Roll out to fit 8-inch pie plate.
