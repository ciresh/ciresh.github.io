Green Bean Casserole Double Batch
2 can (10 3/4 oz.) Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 cup milk
2 tsp. soy sauce
1/4 tsp. Pepper
2 16 oz. frozen french style green beans
2 2/3 cups french fried onions

Cook green beans 5-8 minutes in boiling water.
Heat soup and milk together. Mix soup, milk, soy, pepper, beans and 1 1/3 cup onions in 3-qt. casserole.
BAKE at 350Â°F. for 25 min. or until hot.
STIR and then sprinkle with remaining onions. Bake 5 min.

Potatoes
4 lbs russets
8 tablespoons unsalted butter (1 stick), melted
1 1/2 cup half-and-half, warmed
2 1/4 teaspoons table salt
Ground black pepper

Cook potatoes 20 to 30 minutes. Drain and rice.
Stir in butter with wooden spoon until incorporated; gently whisk in half-and-half, salt.

Gravy (serves 6)
3 cups liquid
3 tablespoons unsalted butter
3 tablespoons unbleached all-purpose flour
2 bay leaves
In medium saucepan, heat butter over medium-high heat; when foaming subsides, add flour and cook, stirring constantly, until flour is dark golden brown and fragrant, about 5 minutes. Whisk in broth mixture and bay leaves and gradually bring to boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionally, until gravy is thick and reduced to 2 cups, 15 to 20 minutes
