Brighams Hot Fudge Sauce
source: Anne Duross (Grammy)

1/4 cup butter
1 1/2 sq unsweetened chocolate
1/4 cup dry cocoa
3/4 cup granulated sugar
dash of salt
1/2 cup all purpose cream
1 tsp vanilla
 
Melt together over low heat. The butter and chocolate. Mix together the sugar, cocoa and salt and add to the chocolate mixture. Mix until smooth. Add cream slowly and bring to boil, stir constantly. 

Remove from heat immediately and add vanilla.

(I Usually double this - it keeps in the 'fridge.) Ann Arcand
