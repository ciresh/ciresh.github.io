Eric Shawarma

1 cup yogurt
1 t Cumin
1/2 Coriander
1 t Smoked paprika
1/2 t Oregano
1/2 t Onion
1/4 t Cinnimon
Juice of half a lemon
1 t salt
4 garlic cloves, pressed
2 lbs. boneless thighs

Mix an marinate for at least 2 hours. Put on skewers and grill
