Instant Pot Polenta Single Serving

1/2 cup water
1/2 cup milk
1 tsp better then bullion
1/4 cups coarse-ground cornmeal
1/2 tablespoons butter

Fill instant pot with 3/4 of inch of water

Mix ingredients into heat profile bool.

Place bowl on trivet in InstantPot. High pressure for 15 minutes, natural release for 15 minutes
