The Best Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe
Coarse sea salt, for garnish

8 ounces unsalted butter (2 sticks; 225g)
1 standard ice cube (about 2 tablespoons; 30mL frozen water)
    
    Cook butter until golden brown, 5 minutes, Add ice cube, cool completely, 20 minutes whisking occasionally 
    
10 ounces all-purpose flour (about 2 cups; 280g)
3/4 teaspoon (3g) baking soda
2 teaspoons Diamond Crystal kosher salt or 1 teaspoon table salt (4g)

    Combine, wisk, set aside
    
5 ounces granulated sugar (about 3/4 cup; 140g)
2 large eggs (100g)
2 teaspoons (10mL) vanilla extract
    
    Whisk in stand mixer until pale brownish hell, and ribbon like, 5 minutes. Swap in paddle.
    
5 ounces dark brown sugar (about 1/2 tightly packed cup plus 2 tablespoons; 140g)
    
    Add to standmixer with butter, mix on medium to combine. Add four mixture until just barely combined

8 ounces (225g) semisweet chocolate, roughly chopped with a knife into 1/2- to 1/4-inch chunks
    Add to mixer, mix until combined.

Use 1oz scoop. Tear balls apart and combine with rough side out. Bake at 325 for 13 to 16 minutes.
