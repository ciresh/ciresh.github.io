Perfect Chocolate Chip Cookies (x1.5)

2 2/3 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
3/4 teaspoon baking soda. Combine and set aside. 

15 Tablespoon butter, browned in skillet (2 minutes) 
6 Tablespoons butter. Combine, pour into large bowl. 

3/4 cup granulated sugar  
1 1/8 cups packed dark brown sugar
1 tablespoon vanilla extract 
1 1/2 teaspoon table salt 
Combine with melted butter

2 large egg, add to bowl. 

Whisk until mixture is smooth with no sugar lumps. Let mixture stand 3 minutes, then whisk for 30 seconds. Repeat 2 more times. Stir in flour mixture

2 cups semisweet chocolate chips. Combine 

Divide into 24 portions (3T or #24 scoop), 8 per sheet. Oven 375. Bake 10-14 minutes (still puffy, edges begun to set).
