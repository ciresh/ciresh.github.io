Maesri Massaman Curry Paste Can Recipe

Stir fry 1 can of curry paste and 300ml of coconumt milk about 5 minutes on medium heat

Add 400g chicken and 200ml of coconut milk and bring to boil while periodically stirring

Add 200g potatoes, 100g of onions and heat until tender. Optional add 2 tbsp roasted peanuts
