Shrimp with Spicy Garlic Paste

2 pounds of Shrimp, brined & deveined
1 large Garlic Clove
1 tsp. Salt
1/2 tsp. Cayenne Pepper
1 tsp. Paprika
2 Tbsp. Olive Oil
2 tsp. Lemon Juice

Mince garlic with salt, mix it with cayenne and paprika in small bowl. Add olive oil and lemon juice to for a thin paste. Toss shrimp with paste until evenly coated. Can be covered and refrigerated up to one hour. Thread shrimp onto skewers if desired. Heat grill or broiler. Grill or broil shrimp, turning once, until shells turn bright pink. 2 to 3 mins per side. Serve hot or at room temp. with lemon wedges.


Shrimp Brining
2 cups kosher salt
2 pounds shrimp

Pour 2 cups of boiling water in a large bowl, add salt and stir until dissolved. Add 3 1/2 quarts cold water (along with some ice if ambient temp is over 70 degrees); stir until salt is completely dissolved. Add shrimp and let stand about 45 min. Drain and rinse thoroughly under cold water. Continue with garlic paste recipe.
