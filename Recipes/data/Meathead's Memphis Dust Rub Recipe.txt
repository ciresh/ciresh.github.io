Meathead's Memphis Dust Rub Recipe

3/4 cup firmly packed dark brown sugar
3/4 cup white sugar
1/2 cup American paprika
1/4 cup garlic powder
2 tablespoons ground black pepper
2 tablespoons ground ginger powder
2 tablespoons onion powder
2 teaspoons rosemary powder (2 to 3 tablespoons of leaves to make 2 teaspoons of powder)

The general rule of thumb is 1/2 teaspoon Morton Coarse Kosher Salt per pound (1 teaspoon diamond)
