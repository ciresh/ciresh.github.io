Fluffy Dinner Rolls

1/2 cup water
3 tablespoons bread flour       Wisk, microwave 20sec increments until stiff (40-80)

1/2 cup cold milk               Mix with paste till smooth
1 large egg                     Incorporate

2 cups (11oz) bread flour       Added to wet.    
1 1/2 teaspoons yeast           Mix low with dough hook 1-2 minutes. Let stand 15 min

2 tablespoons sugar
1 teaspoon salt                 Mix medium-low for 5 minutes

4 T u-butter, softened          Add 1 T at time. Mix for 5 minutes

Knead briefly. Place in greased bowl 1 hour (2x size). 
Knead slightly for into 8x9 rectangle. Div 8/4 and 9/3
Stretch to 2x8 and roll tightly. Put 10 around 9" greased
Cakepan. 2 in center. Let rise 1h-45m.

Bake 375 for 25-30 minutes. Cool 3 minutes, remove and brush with 1/2T melted
