Instant Pot Cilantro Lime Rice

1 cup long-grain white rice no rinsed
1 1/4 cup water
2 tablespoon olive oil divided
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
salt to taste
1/3 cup fresh cilantro
3 tablespoon fresh lime juice

Place the rice, 1 tablespoon of oil, salt, garlic powder and water in the Instant Pot and stir well.

Close the lid and cook on high pressure for 6 minutes then 10 minutes (Original had 3 minutes and nature release).

In a bowl mix the 1 tablespoon of olive oil, lime juice and minced cilantro. Add the mixture to the rice and stir.

Serve with an additional lime slice and enjoy!
