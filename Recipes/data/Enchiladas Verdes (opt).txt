Enchiladas Verdes

2 teaspoons oil. Heat in medium pan.
1 medium onion, chopped medium. Cook until golden.
2 medium garlic cloves, pressed.
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin. Combine. Cook 30 seconds
1 1/2 cups low-sodium chicken broth. 
1 lb boneless chicken (2-3 breasts). Combine. Cook to 160 15-20 minutes 
1/4 cup liquid. Reserve

1 1/2 pounds tomatillos (or 3 11-oz cans)
3 poblano, halved, stemmed.
2 teaspoons oil. Toss. Broil until blackend & start to soften (5-10 min). Cool 10 minutes.
1 teaspoon sugar, salt, & garlic. Reserved liquid. Remove poblano skins. Process until chunky (8 1 sec pulses).

Shred cooled chicken. 
1/2 cup chopped cilantro. 
1 1/2 cups Monterey Jack cheese. Mix

3/4 tomatillo sauce on bottom of 13x9 pan
10-12 6" tortillas. Fill with 1/3 cup filling
1/2 cup cheese. Top. Cover with foil and bake 450 15-20 minutes
