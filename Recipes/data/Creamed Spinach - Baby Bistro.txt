Creamed Spinach

1 tablespooon olive oil 
8 ounces spinach
2 tablespoon butter
2 tablespoon all-purpose flour
1 small onion, chopped fine
1 1/2 cups milk
1/4 teaspoon salt

In large skillet, heat oil over meduim-high heat. Add the spinach and cook, quickly working the spinach down with tongs until cooked. Remove spinach cool and chopped. Add butter to pan. Cook onion until soft. Add flour and cook for 5 minutes or until bubbly. Add milk and cooks for 5 minutes
