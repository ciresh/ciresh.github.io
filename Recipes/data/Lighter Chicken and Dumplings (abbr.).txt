Lighter Chicken and Dumplings (abbr.)

6 chicken thighs (skin and bones 2 1/2 pounds, trimmed) seasoned with 1 t salt and Â¼ t pepper (sub 2 chicken breast if desired) 
2 teaspoons vegetable oil
    Cook in hot oil, skin side down 5-7 minutes, flip, 5-7 longer. Remove chicken and all but 1 t fat.
2 small onions, chopped fine (about 1 1/2 cups)
2 medium carrots, peeled and cut into 3/4-inch pieces (about 2 cups)
1 celery rib, medium, chopped fine (about 1/2 cup)
    Add to pot, stir occasionally 7-9 minutes
1/4 cup dry sherry
    Add, scrape up brown bits
6 cups low-sodium chicken broth
1 teaspoon minced fresh thyme leaves
    Add along with chicken, simmer 45-55 minutes until done.
1 pound chicken wings (see note)
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley leaves
    Remove chicken then skin, pull off meat and chop to 1 inch pieces
    Allow pot to cool for 5 minutes. Skim fat. Add chicken back
    
2 cups unbleached all-purpose flour (10 ounces)
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon sugar
1 teaspoon table salt
    Combine and whisk.
3/4 cup buttermilk, cold (see note)
4 tablespoons (1/2 stick) unsalted butter, melted and cooled about 5 minutes
1 large egg white
    Combine buttermilk and butter, stirring until butter forms small clumps; whisk in egg white
    Add buttermilk mixture to dry ingredients and stir with rubber spatula until just incorporated and batter pulls away from sides of bowl.
    Use greased tablespoon (or #60 disher), drop over top of stew, spacing about 1/4 inch apart (~ 24 dumplings). Wrap lid of Dutch oven with clean kitchen towel.
    Simmer gently until dumplings have doubled in size and toothpick inserted into center comes out clean, 13 to 16 minutes.
