American Sandwich Bread

3 1/2 cups bread flour
2 teaspoons salt
1 cup warm milk (110 degrees)
1/3 cup warm water (110 degrees)
2 tablespoons butter, melted
3 tablespoons honey
1 package (about 2 1/4 teaspoons) rapid rise yeast

Heat oven to 200 degree for 10 minutes, turn off

Mix flour and salt in stand mixer. Mix milk, water, butter, honey and yeast on bowl. Turn mixer on low and add wet ingredients. Mix on medium until smooth and satiny, 10 minutes.

Place in oil container and coat dough. Put in oven and let raise until double in size, 40-50 minutes.

Press dough in rectangle, 1 inch thick and no longer than 9 inches. With long side facing you, roll into cylinder, pressing with your fingers to make sure dough sticks to itself. Turn dough seam side up and pinch it closed. Place dough in greased 9x5x3 loaf pan and press gently so it touches all sides. Cover with plastic wrap and set aside in warm spot until doubles in size, 20 to 30 minutes. 

Heat oven to 350 degrees and place empty loaf pan on bottom rack.. Bring 2 cups water to boil.

Place pan with dough in oven and pour heated water into empty loaf pan. Bake until loaf reads 195 degrees, 40 to 50 minutes.
