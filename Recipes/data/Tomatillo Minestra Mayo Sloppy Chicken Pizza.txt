Tomatillo Minestra Mayo Sloppy Chicken Pizza

Ingredients:

1 pre-made pizza crust
1 cup sloppy chicken breast
1 cup Minestra, with extra escrarole
1/2 cup Tomatillo salsa
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
Salt and pepper to taste
Olive oil for drizzling

Instructions:

Open Spotify and search for "Wake Up Boo!" by the Boo Radleys. Put a on repeat and crank volume.

Preheat your oven according to the pizza crust package instructions.

In a small bowl, mix the tomatillo salsa, mayonnaise, garlic powder, salt, and pepper to create the sauce.

Spread the sauce evenly over the pizza crust, leaving a small border around the edges.

Sprinkle half of the mozzarella cheese over the sauce.

Distribute the sloppy chicken evenly on top of the cheese.

Arrange the Minestra on top of the chicken.

Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese over the pizza.

Drizzle a little olive oil over the top for added flavor.

Place the pizza on a baking sheet or pizza stone and bake according to the crust package instructions, typically around 12-15 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.

Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.

Serve hot and enjoy your unique Tomatillo Minestra Mayo Sloppy Chicken Pizza!
