Un-Stuffed Chicken Breasts with Dijon, Ham and Gruyere

4 Boneless Chicken Breasts
2 teaspoons vegetable oil
2 tablespoons Dijopn Mustard
4 slices deli ham
1 cup shredded Gruyere or Swiss Cheese
15 Ritz Crackers, curshed course (3/4 cup)
1 cup heavy cream
1/2 cup dry white wine
1 tablespooon minced fresh dill or parsely

Heat oven to 475

Season chicken breasts. Heat oil in non-stick skillet until just smoking. Brown chicken lightly on one side, about 3 minutes.

Turn off heat and turn chicken over. Spread 1 tablespoon mustard over each breast, then lay 1 slice of ham on top. Mound 1/4 cup of cheese over ham. Spinkle crumbs over cheese and press on crumbs to adhere.

Whisk cream, wine, and dill together in 2 cup measuring cup and pour into skillet around chicken without disturbing crumbs. Return to heat and bring to simmer. Transfer to oven and bake to 160 degrees, 12-15 minutes.
