Heart Pan Chocolate Cakes

5 oz bittersweet chocolate, chopped
4 Tablespoons (1/2 stick) unsalted butter, cut into cubes and softened
1 Tablespoon cognac
2 eggs + 2egg yolks
1/4 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/8 tsp salt
1 Tablespoon flour + 1 tsp flour

Preheat oven to 400F and butter wells of heart muffin pan. In a small saucepan over low heat, combine chocolate and butter, cook, stirring occasionally, until the mixture is melted, smooth and blended, 2-3 minutes and remove from heat, Stir in cognac until blended and let cool 10 minutes. In the bowl of an electric mixer combine eggs, eggs yolks, vanilla and salt. Beat ingredients and high speed until thick and ribbony, 5-6 minutes. Remove bowl from the mixer, sift the flour over bowl and fold gently until just blended. Add the chocolate mixture to the batter and fold together to form a smooth, blended batter. Divide batter among buttered wells of the muffin pan and bake until tops are firm, but centers of cakes are moist 9 to 11 minutes.
