Foolproof Pan Pizza

1 (28-ounce) can whole peeled tomatoes
    Process util pureed with immersion blender, food process or blender
1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
1 tablespoon unsalted butter
    Melt over medium-low heat in medium sauce pan
2 medium cloves garlic, grated on microplane grater (about 2 teaspoons)
1 teaspoon dried oregano
pinch red pepper flakes
Kosher salt
    Add to pan, stirring frequently, until fragrant but not browned, about 3 minutes       
1 medium yellow onion, peeled and split in half
1 teaspoon sugar
    Add with tomatoes. Simmer untile reduced by 1/2, about 1 hour    

14 ounces, (2 1/2 cups) bread flour
2 teaspoons kosher salt, plus more for sprinkling
1/2 teaspoon instant yeast
9.5 ounces (1 cup plus 3 tablespoons) water
2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil, plus more to coat pans and drizzle
    Combine in large bowl. Cover, let sit 8-24 hours
    Sprinkle with flour, divide in 2 halves
1 tablespoons of oil 
    Put oil in pan, then dough. Push out dough to edges. Cover and let rise 2 hour
1 1/2 cups pizza sauce
12 ounces grated full-fat, dry mozzarella cheese (see note above)
    Pop any bubbles and push out dough to edges. Put 1/2 sauce and cheese on dough.
    Cook in 550 degree oven 12-15 minutes
