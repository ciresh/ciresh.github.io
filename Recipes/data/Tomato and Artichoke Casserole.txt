Tomato and Artichoke Casserole
Winners (Page: 133)

Exotic but easy.

Ingredients:
5 Tbsp. butter
1 large onion, chopped
3 cups bread crumbs
1/2 tsp. sugar
1/2 - 1 tsp.salt
1 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. thyme
1 tsp. basil
1 - 14 oz. (398 ml) can artichoke hearts, cut in quarters
4 medium sized tomatoes, thickly sliced
2 Tbsp. butter, melted

Instructions:
Fry onion in butter until transparent. Mix in bread crumbs, sugar, salt, pepper, thyme and basil. Remove from heat. Butter a 1 qt. casserole dish and fill with alternate layers of artichokes, tomatoes and onion-bread crumb mixture, ending with bread crumbs. Drizzle butter over top. Bake at 375 F. for 45 minutes. Serves 6.
