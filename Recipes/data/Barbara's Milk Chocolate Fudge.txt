5 cups sugar
1 tall can evaporated milk
pinch of salt
6 tbsp butter
12 oz package semi-sweet chocholate morsels
16 oz milk chocolate bar cut into pieces
1 cup Fluff
2 teaspoons vanilla
2 cups walnuts
Combine chocolate chips and milk chocholate in a large bowl
Lightly butter 13x9 pan
Combine sugar, milk, salt and butter in a 3 quart sauce pan. Stirring mixture constantly bring to rolling boil. Start timing at this point; Boil for 6 minutes continuing to stir. Remove from heat, pour into bowl with chocolart pieces and stir until smooth. Add fluff, walnuts and vanilla, stirring to blend. quickly poir into buttered pan, swirling the top of the fudge.
