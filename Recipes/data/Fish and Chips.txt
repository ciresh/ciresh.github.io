Fish and Chips

1 cup flour
1/2 cup cornstarch
1 t Baking powder
1 t Onion Powder
1 t Garlic Powder
1 t Lawry's
8-16 ounces Lager Beer (used Yuengling)

Combine with enough beer to make a loose pancake like consistency

1/2 cup flour
1-2 lbs White Fish (Cod and Tilapia worked well)

Dredge filets in flour, then dip in batter. Fry till well cooked.

Tarter Sauce

3 T Mayo
2 t Sweet Relish
1 T Capers, chopped
1 t Dijon mustard
1 T finely diced red onion
1 t White vinegar
