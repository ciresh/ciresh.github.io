Soft Polenta

2 quarts water
2 teaspoons salt
2 cups coarse-ground cornmeal
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons minced parsley

Combine water, salt cornmeal and butter in 3 to 4 quart over proof saucepan. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour 20 minutes. Remove from oven and set aside 5 minutes to rest before serving.
To serve, spoon polenta into each of 6 warmed shallow pasta bowls. Place one rib on each and spoon ragout over top. Garnish with parsley. Serve immediately. Makes 6 servings.
