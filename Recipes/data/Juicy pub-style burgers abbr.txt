Juicy pub-style burgers

2 lb steak tips, trimmed and cut into 1/2-inch chunks, freeze 35m minutes
Quarter batches, grind to 1/16-inch pieces, about 35 one-second pulses. Remove any grisle
4T unsalted butter, mixed in with fork
Gently make into patties, 3/4 inch thick and about 4 1/2 inches in diameter
1 teaspoon vegetable oil in skillet, until smoking.
Cook 2 minutes each side
Put on baking sheet in 300 degree over.
Cook until 125 degrees for medium-rare or 130 degrees for medium, 3 to 6 minutes.
Cool 5 minutes
