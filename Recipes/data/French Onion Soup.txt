French Onion Soup

Full stock
2700g (3 boxes/11c) beef stock
900g (2lb) 90/10 ground beef

Quick stock
2700g beef stock
30g (2T) BTB beef
3 packets gelatin

Soup
2500g onions (7 med), sliced
115g butter
60g (1/4c) water
salt
~1000g (4c) water for deglazing
2g thyme
5g black pepper
10g flour
150g dry sherry
25g Worcestershire
2500g beef stock
0.5g MSG opt

Cheesy toast
8 slices sourdough
30g oil/butter
150g Gruyère
8 slices provolone

Instructions

Pressure-cook beef + stock 25–30 min, strain (or whisk quick stock)
Slice onions into 1.5" strips
Pot on med-high add butter, onions, water, big pinch salt; cover 5 min
Stir, uncover, cook 5 more min until soft and lightly colored
Lower to med, cook 30 min stirring and scraping often
As fond builds, lower heat slightly and deglaze repeatedly with water until onions are deep brown and jammy
Add thyme, pepper, flour; stir 30 sec
Add sherry, reduce 3–5 min until nearly dry
Add Wuster, stock, msg; simmer 15 min
Season aggressively with salt
Fry bread, top with cheeses, broil until bubbly
Serve soup with 1–2 cheesy toasts and black pepper
