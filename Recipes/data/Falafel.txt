Falafel

8 Ounces dried chickpeas, picked and rinsed
3/4 cup fresh cilantro leaves and stems
3/4 cup fresh parsley leaves
1/2 onion, chopped fine
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
2 teaspoon backing powder
2 quarts vegetable oil

Tahini Sauce

1/3 cup tahini
1/3 cup plain Greek yogurt
1/4 cup lemon juice
1/4 cup water
Salt

Soak chick peas for 8-24 hours. Drain well

Tahini sauce: Whisk all ingredients except salt and water until smooth. Whisk in water to desired consistency. 

 Process cilantro, parsley, onion garlic, coriander, cumin, salt and cayenne in food processor 5 seconds. Scrape down side of the bowl. Continue to process until mixture resembles pesto, about 5 seconds longer. Add chickpeas and pulse 6 times. Scrape down side of bowl. Continue to pulse until chickpeas are coarsely chopped and resemble sesame seeds, about 6 more pulses. Transfer mixture to large bowl and set aside.
 
 Whisk flour and 1/3 cup water in bowl until no lumps remain. microwave, whisking every 10 second until mixtures thicken ti stiff, smooth, pudding-like consistency that forms mound when dropped end of whisk into, 40 to 80 seconds. Stir baking powder int flour paste. 
 
 Add flour paste to chickpea mixture and using rubber spatula mix until fully incorporated. Divided mixture into 24 pieces and gently roll into gold pall-sized spheres, transferring spheres to parchment paper lines rimmed baking sheet once formed (can be refrigerated for 2 hours).
 
 Fry half the falafel at time, for about 5 minutes.
 
Notes: Use the small scoop (#40 I think, about 4 oz mixture)
