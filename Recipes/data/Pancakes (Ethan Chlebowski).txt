Pancakes (Ethan Chlebowski)

Wet Ingredients 

113 g plain yogurt + 113 g water (8 oz total / 226 g) - 2 parts LIQUID
2 large eggs (~113 g) - 1 part EGG
56 g vegetable oil - 1/2 part FAT
3 g vanilla (optional)

Dry Ingredients 

226 g all purpose flour - 2 parts FLOUR
30 g white sugar
12 g baking powder
5 g salt
1 g ground cinnamon - optional
1 g fresh ground nutmeg - optional

Combine and whisk the wet ingredients in a bowl until thoroughly combined.

In a separate bowl, combine the dry ingredients. Sift out any big clumps.

Combine the wet and dry ingredients together and stir until the batter is smooth and light, do not over mix it or the batter can become tough.

Adjust the batter, if you want it thinner, add a little more water.

Add any whole ingredients like nuts, chocolate, or fruit at this stage.

Cook the pancakes on an oiled griddle over medium heat.
