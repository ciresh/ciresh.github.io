Indian Spiced Roasted Chickpeas

2 (15oz) cans chickpeas
3 Tablespoons Extra virgin olive oil
2 teaspoons paprika
1 teaspoon coriander
1/2 teaspoon turmeric
1/2 teaspoon allspice
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/2 teaspoon sugar
1/8 teaspoon kosher salt
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Preheat oven to 350
Drain chickpeas for 10 minutes. Line plate with double layer of paper towels and spread out chickpeas onto even layer. Microwave until dry and many have split slightly at the seams, 8 to 12 minutes.
Transfer chickpeas to 13 x 9 metal baking pan. Add oil and stir until even coated. Roast for 30 minutes. 
While roasting, combine spices.
Stir chickpeas to center of pan, continue to roast until dry and slightly shriveled, and deep golden brown, 20 to 40 minutes longer.
Transfer to large bowl, toss with spice mixture, let cool 30 minutes

BBQ variation- Omit allspice and cumin. Substitute 1 tablespoon smoked paprika, for paprika, garlic powder for coriander, and onion powder for turmeric. Increase sugar to 1 1/2 tablespoon.
