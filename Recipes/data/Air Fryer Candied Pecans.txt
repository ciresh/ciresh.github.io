Air Fryer Candied Pecans

1/4 cup light brown sugar
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1 tablespoon maple syrup
1 tablespoon water
2 cups pecan halves
1/4 teaspoon salt

Line the air fryer basket with parchment paper or foil.

In a medium bowl toss brown sugar, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, maple syrup, water, pecan, and salt until well coated.

Pour the mixture inside the air fryer basket and air fry to 330ÂºF for 8 minutes and shake the basket halfway through. Cook the nuts until 
shiny and fragrant.

Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or silpat and spread the air fried nut in an even layer. Let it cool for at least 10 minutes before serving. .
