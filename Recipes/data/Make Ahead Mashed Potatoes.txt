Make Ahead Mashed Potatoes

5 lb russets (9 medium)
3 cups heavy cream
8 tablespoons unsalted butter

Microwave on high for 16 minutes turning half way through

Bake at 450 for 30 minutes and soft turning half way through

Scoop out potatoes and place half in stand mixer

Beat on high speed till smooth (30 seconds) the gradually add remaining potatoes (1-2 minutes)

Fold in 2 cups creams, butter and 2 teaspoons salt

Add cream (up to 1/2 cup) to desired serving consistency. Then add additional 1/2 cup

To reheat, microwave at 75% for 14 till hot, stirring occasionally.
