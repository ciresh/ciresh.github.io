Kofta Kebabs (Erics Mod)

3 slice whole grain bread, toasted
2 medium yellow onion, grated
2/3 cup chopped Italian parsley
2/3 cup chopped cilantro
12 cloves garlic, minced
3 lb Ground Beef

1 1/2 tablespoons kosher salt
1 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
1 1/2 teaspoon paprika
3/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
3/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
3/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon sumac
1/2 teaspoon coriander
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika

Put break in food processor and process until fine. Add garlic, parsley and cilantro. Process until a fine paste

Mix the beef with the bread crumbs and herbs.

Combine the salt and spices into a shaker. Sprinkle 1/3 over the meat and mix to incorporate. Repeat with the remaining spices. Microwave a small piece and taste for seasoning.

Wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate until chilled, at least 1 hour, up to overnight.

Remove meat from the refrigerator and divide into 12 pieces (around 110 grams earch) Shape meat into a uniform log, about 6 inches long and 2 inches wide.

Meanwhile, preheat a charcoal grill until coals are very hot.

Place kebabs on the hot grill until browned and cooked through, about 12 minutes, turning every 3 minutes or so. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center of a kebab should read at least 145 degrees F (63 degrees C). Serve.

Note: May want to add 1% salt
