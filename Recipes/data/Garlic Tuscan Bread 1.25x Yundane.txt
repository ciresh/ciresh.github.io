Garlic Tuscan Bread 1.25x Yundane

65g Flour
65g Boiling Water

385g bread flour (or 435g all purpose)
50g durum wheat flour (semolina)
400g water
1 1/4 tsp sea salt (~7.5g)
1/4 tsp yeast (~3g)
3/8 tsp amylase enzyme powder (~1.25g)
3/8 tsp ascorbic acid (vitamin c) (~1.25g)
3/8 tsp diastic malt powder (~1.25g) (or 1 tsp w/o amylase)
3/8 tsp soy lecithin (~1.25g)
garlic oil for topping

Dissolve durum wheat flour in water. Added additional ingredients and mix well. Let rest one hour. With wet hands, fold dough and put in fridge overnight.

Fold dough to degas. Press dough and roll up to shape. Proof covered until doubled in size (1-2 hours).

Bake covered 420f for 30 minutes. Lid off to 10-15 minutes until reaches desired brownness.

Brush with garlic oil while warm.
