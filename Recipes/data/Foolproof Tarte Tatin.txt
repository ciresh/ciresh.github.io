Foolproof Tarte Tatin

7-inch ovenproof skillet

Apples
3 medium or 4 Pink Lady or any sweet-tart, firm baking apples (1.5 lb / .68 kg)
1/3 cup maple syrup (3.5 oz / 100g)
1/6 cup brandy (1.3 oz /37g), preferably apple brandy
1 teaspoons apple cider vinegar (0.15 oz / 4g)
1/4 teaspoon plus a pinch of Diamond Crystal kosher salt

Caramel
1/2 cup sugar (3.5 oz /100g)
1.5 tablespoons unsalted butter (.75 oz / 22g), cut into 1/2-inch pieces

Rough Puff Pastry
3 oz unsalted butter, chilled
0.875 cups all-purpose flour (4 oz / 110g), plus more for rolling
1 teaspoon sugar
Pinch Diamond Crystal kosher salt (0.16 oz / 5g)


Roast Apples 275 check after 1-1.25 hours till tender.

Freeze butter 10-15 minutes.

425 for 20 minutes, then 350 for 35-45 minutes.
