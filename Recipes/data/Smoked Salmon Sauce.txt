Smoked Salmon Vodka Cream Sauce
Submitted by: Fatima
A rich and creamy sauce that will compliment your favorite pasta. Easy 
to double or triple if you need more than 2 servings worth. Serve with 
your favorite pasta.
Servings: 2

Ingredients:
1 tablespoon butter
1 tablespoon finely diced onion
1/2 cup diced smoked salmon
2 fluid ounces vodka
1/4 cup heavy whipping cream
1/4 cup chopped tomatoes
1/2 cup tomato sauce
 salt to taste
 ground black pepper to taste
1 pinch ground nutmeg
1/8 tablespoon cayenne pepper



Directions:
1. In a large saucepan saute butter and onion with smoked salmon. Once 
the pan begins to lightly smoke, pull saucepan away from heat source 
and add the Vodka.
2. When the Vodka has burned off, return the sauce to heat source and 
mix in the tomatoes, tomato sauce and heavy cream.
3. Season sauce with salt, pepper, nutmeg, and chile pepper. Reduce 
sauce to desired consistency; serve.
