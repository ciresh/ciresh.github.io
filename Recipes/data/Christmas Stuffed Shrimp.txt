Baked Stuffed Shrimp with Crabmeat Stuffing Recipe

20 Shrimp

1 lb fresh lump crabmeat, shells removed (or 2 cans of canned crabmeat if fresh isn't available)
4 tablespoons butter, plus
3 tablespoons melted butter
1/2 cup onion, minced
1/4 cup celery, minced
1 tablespoon garlic, minced
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley (optional)
1/4 cup mayonnaise 
1 egg, beaten
3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice (optional)
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce (optional)
1 cup buttery cracker, crushed (about 22)
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon fresh ground black pepper
2 lbs u12 shrimp or jumbo shrimp, shelled,deveined,and butterflied
6 20-21 Shrimp (optional, process with vegetables

Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.

In your fry pan, melt 4 tablespoons butter over medium-high heat.

Add the onions, celery, and cook, stirring, until softened, about 4 minutes.

Add the parsley and the garlic, and cook, stirring, for 1 minute.

Remove from heat and let cool.

Add the cooled vegetables to the crabmeat and toss to combine.

Add the mayonnaise, egg, and stir gently.

Add 1 cup of cracker crumbs, salt and pepper to taste, and stir gently, being careful not to break up the crabmeat.
Spoon the crabmeat stuffing evenly into each shrimp (about 2 Tbsp or use #30 or #40 scoop), and place stuffed sides up, in your baking dish.

Drizzle with the remaining melted butter. Bake until golden, about 20 minutes.
