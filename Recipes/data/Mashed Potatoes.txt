Mashed Potatoes

INGREDIENTS
2 pounds Russets
4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
Table salt
2/3 cup half and half, warm
Ground black pepper

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Peel and quarter potatoes. Cover with water and cook till tender. Drain water and heat over low heat until all liquid is evaporated.

2. Set ricer or food mill over now-empty pot. Working in batches, transfer potatoes to hopper of ricer or food mill and process, removing any potatoes stuck to bottom. Using rubber spatula, stir in melted butter and 1/2 teaspoon salt until incorporated. Stir in half and half until incorporated. Season to taste with salt and pepper; serve immediately.
