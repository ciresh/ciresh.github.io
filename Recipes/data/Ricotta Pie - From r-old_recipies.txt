Ricotta Pie - From r-old_recipies

9 inch pie crust

2 egg yolks
1 cup shortening
1/2 tsp salt
1-2 tbs cold water
? flour

3 cups (1 1/2 lbs) Ricotta Cheese
1/4 cup flour
2 tbs grated orange peel
2 tbs grated lemon peel
1 tbs vanilla extract
1/8 tbs salt

beat until foamy
add gradually
4 eggs
1 cup sugar

Stir eggs and sugar into Ricotta mixture until well blended and smooth. Pou Ricotta into pastry

Bake 350f 50-60 minu untilmixutre is firm and crust is brown
