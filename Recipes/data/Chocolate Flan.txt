Chocolate Flan

Caramel:
150g/5.5oz White Sugar
50ml/3tbsp Water

For the Flan:
1 Egg + 3 Egg Yolks 
300ml/14fl oz Condensed Milk
250ml/8.5fl oz Milk
4g/1Tsp Vanilla Extract
30g/1oz Cocoa Powder

Make caramel, coat 6 oz ramekins.

Mix ingredients Bake covered with foil in water bath at 360 degrees for 40 minutes
