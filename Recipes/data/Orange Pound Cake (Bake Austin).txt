Orange Pound Cake (Bake Austin)

2 sticks butter (room temp)
8 oz cream cheese (room temp)
3 cups sugar
6 eggs
3 cups flour
1/8 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla
Orange zest from 2 oranges

Preheat oven to 300F and g rease 2 loaf pans and set aside.

In a mixing bowl cream butter, cream cheese, and sugar until smooth and fluffy.

Add one egg at a time and mix until combined. Add flour, vanilla, salt, and orange zest and mix until just combined.

Fill the loaf pans 3/4 full Bake for approx. 40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.

Once the poundcake is cooled you can cover it with a glaze. To make a glaze: Mix 1 cup powdered sugar with 2-3 Tbsp orange juice.
Spread over the poundcake.
