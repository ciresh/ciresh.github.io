Buttermilk Cupcakes

3 cups cake flour
1t baking powder
1/2t baking soda
1/2t salt
1 1/2 (3 sticks) unsalted butter, at room temp
2 cups sugar
4 large eggs
2t vanilla extract
1 1/3 cups buttermilk

Preheat oven to 350. Line 24 cupcakes tins with baking papers. Sift together the flour, b. powder, b. soda and salt. Set aside.

In the bowl of an electric mixer, cream together the butter and sugar until fluffy, about 3 minutes. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla. Alternately add the flour mixture and buttermilk, beginning and ending with the flour.

Fill each baking cup 2/3 full. Bake until a skewer inserted into the middle of a cupcake comes out clean, about 20 minutes.


Swiss Meringue Buttercream

4 large egg whites
1 cup sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
2 cups (4 sticks) unsalted butter, at room temp, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

In a double boiler set over simmering water, whisk together the egg whites, sugar, salt and cream of tartar. Continue whisking until the mixture becomes too hot to touch comfortably, 7 to 10 minutes.

Transfer the mixture to an electric mixer fitted with a whisk attachment. Beat on med-high speed until the egg whites hold a stiff peak, about 8 minutes. Add the butter, one or two pieces at a time, beating after each addition until incorporated. Beat in the vanilla. Use at room temperature.

Makes 3 cups, enough to frost 24 cupcakes.
