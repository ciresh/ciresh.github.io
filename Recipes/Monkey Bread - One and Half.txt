Monkey Bread

1 1/2 cup milk, warm (about 110 degrees)
1/3c + 2T + 1-3/4t  warm water (was 1/3)
1/3c + 2t granulated sugar (was 1/4)            
3 tablespoons melted u-butter
1 package + 1t rapid-rise yeast (or instant)    Combine

4 3/4 cup + 2 tablespoons flour (was 3 1/4 c)
1T table salt                                   Mix with dough hook. Added wet. Mix 6-7 minute. Let rise 50-70
