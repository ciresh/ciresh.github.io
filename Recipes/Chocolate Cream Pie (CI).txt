Chocolate Cream Pie

1/3 cup (2 1/3 ounce) sugar
1/4 cup (1-ounce) cornstarch
2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
3 cups whole milk or 2 percent low-fat
6 ounce bittersweet chocolate, chopped fine
3 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut into 3 pieces
2 teaspoon vanilla extract

1 cup heavy cream
1 tablespoon confectioner's sugar

Roll dough into 9" pan, trim 1/4 inch to edge then tuck underneath. Refrigerate for  30 minutes. Preheat oven to 350 Line pie with foil and pie weights and cover edges. Bake for 15-20 minutes just until edges turning brown. Remove foil and bake for 15-20 minutes until golden brown. 

Whisk sugar, cornstarch, cocoa and salt in sauce pan. Whisk in milk and cook until thicken and bubbling over entire surface, 8-10 minutes. Add chocolate and butter and stir to incorporate. Whisk in vanilla and pour into crust. Cover with greased parchment paper and let cool 1 hour. Place in fridge for 2 1/2-24 hours. 

Dream Creamy Filling:
1/2 cup Sugar 
4 1/2 tbsp Cornstarch 
1 1/2 cup (9oz) Semisweet chocolate chips 
3 cups Milk 
1 1/2 tsp Vanilla extract
