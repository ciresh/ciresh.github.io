Really Good Garlic Bread

1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon water
8 tablespoon unsalted butter
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
4-5 garlic cloves, minced to a paste (1 tablespoon)
1 (1-pound) loaf soft Italian bread. Halfed horizontally

Preheat over to 450 degrees. Combine garlic powder and water in medium bowl. Add 4 tablespoons butter, salt and cayenne to bowl, set aside.

Place remaining 4 tablespoon butter in small bowl and microwave, covered, until melted abouit 30 seconds. Stir in garlic and continue to microwave until mixture is bubbling around edges, about 1 minutes, stirring half way through. Transfer melted butter mixture to bowl with garlic powder-butter mixture and whisk until homogeneous loose past forms.

Spread cut sides of bread evenly with butter mixture, Transfer bread, cut side up, to rimmed baking sheet. Bake until butter mixture has melted, 3-4 minutes. Remove sheet from oven. Flip bread cut side down place second rimmed backing sheet on top and gently press. Return sheet to oven, leaving second sheet on top of bread and continue to bake until cut sides are golden brown and crisp, 4 to 12 minutes longer, rotating sheet halfway through baking.
