Perfect Chocolate Chip Cookies

1 3/4 cups unbleached all-purpose flour (8 3/4 ounces)
1/2 teaspoon baking soda. Combine and set aside.

10 Tablespoon butter, browned in skillet (2 minutes)
4 Tablespoons butter. Combine, pour into large bowl.

1/2 cup granulated sugar (3 1/2 ounces)
3/4 cups packed dark brown sugar (5 1/4 ounces) (see note)
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1 teaspoon table salt
Combine with melted butter

1 large egg & 1 large egg yolk, add to bowl.

Whisk until mixture is smooth with no sugar lumps. Let mixture stand 3 minutes, then whisk for 30 seconds. Repeat 2 more times. Stir in flour mixture

1 1/4 cups semisweet chocolate chips. Combine

Divide into 16 portions (3T or #24 scoop), 8 per sheet. Oven 375. Bake 10-14 minutes (still puffy, edges begun to set).
