Monique's Pumpkin Ice Cream

1 c Canned, unsweetened pumpkin
1/3 c Dark Brown Sugar
1 T Ground Cinnamon
1/4 t Ground Nutmeg
1/8 t Groung Cloves
1/2 t Ground Ginger

Follow Dessert Bible's vanilla ice. Combine all additional ingredient well then add to egg and cream mixture before freezing. Note that you will have to make the Icecream in batches.
