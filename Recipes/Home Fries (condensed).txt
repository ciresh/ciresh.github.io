Home Fries

Heat oven to 500 with rimmed baking sheet. Bring 10 cups water to boil.
  3 1/2 pounds russet potatoes 3/4 dice
  1/2 teaspoon baking soda
Add ingredient and return to boil, cook 1 minute, drain, return to pan, dry over low heat 2 minutes
  3 tablespoons unsalted butter , cut into 12 pieces
  1 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
Mix 30 seconds with rubber spatula
  2 tablespoons vegetable oil
Add oil to sheet, add potatoes, roast 15 minutes
  2 onions, cut into 1/2-inch dice
  1 tablespoons vegetable oil
  1/2 teaspoon kosher salt 
Combine. Turn potatoes. Add onions to 8 by 5-inch space in center of baking sheet. Roast for 15 minutes.
Mix in onions, roast until potatoes are well browned and onions are softened and beginning to brown, 5 to 10 minutes
