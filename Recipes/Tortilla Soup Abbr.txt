Tortilla Soup
Serves 4 to 6

1 large dried pasilla (negro) chile
One 15-ounce can diced tomatoes in juice (preferably fire-roasted)
    Toast chile over open flame for a few seconds, stem and seed and place in blender with tomatoes
    
2 tablespoons vegetable or olive oil
1 medium white onion, sliced 1/4-inch thick
3 garlic cloves, peeled
    Saute 7 minutes, add to blender and puree.  Heat pan to medium-high and puree and cook 6 minutes.

2 quarts chicken broth
1 large epazote sprig, if you have one
    Add broth and simmer medium-low 15 minutes
1 1/4 pounds chicken cut into 1/2-inch cubes
    Add chicken, cook 5 minutes.
