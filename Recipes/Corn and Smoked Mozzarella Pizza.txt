Corn and Smoked Mozzarella Pizza
Cooking Light 2003 (dough omitted)

2 ears shucked corn
Cooking Spray
2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
1/4 crushed red bell pepper flakes
1 clove garlic, minced
1 cup (4 ounces) shredded smoked mozzarella
1 cup very thinly slicked red onion
2 tablespoons chopped chives
1 1/2 teaspoons grated lime rind

1. Prepare grill or broiler/Preheat oven to 500

2. Place corn on grill rack or broiler pan coated with cooking spray, cook 10 minutes, turning occasionally, Cool. Cut kernels from corn to measure 1 cup; set aside.

3. Place 1 tablespoon oil and red pepper in a small bowl; microware HIGH 30 seconds. Stir in garlic; set aside.

4. Roll out dough and Brush with evenly with oil mixture and sprinkle with cheese. Top evenly with corn and onion, and sprinkle with 1/2 teaspoon salt. Bake at 500 for 8 minutes or until golden. Sprinkle with chives and lime rind
