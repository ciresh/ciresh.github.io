Smoked Sausage with Cabbage and Sweet Potatoes

1 (1-lb.) pkg. Hillshire FarmÂ® Smoked Sausage (any variety), sliced
13 1/4-oz. chicken broth
1 Tbsp. mustard, prepared
1 tsp. mustard seeds (optional)
1 lb. sweet potatoes, peeled and sliced
2/3 cup onion, coarsely chopped
1 1/4 lbs. (6 packed cups) green cabbage, shredded coarse
1 large golden delicious apple or other firm apple, cored and sliced 

Directions:

Stir chicken broth and mustard in deep 12" skillet. Add mustard seeds and bring to a boil.

Add sweet potatoes and onions. Reduce heat to medium. Cover and cook about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Add sausage and cabbage. Cover and cook about 12 minutes longer, stirring once or twice, until potatoes are tender and cabbage is crisp-tender.

Stir in apple. Cover and simmer additional 5 minutes until apple is crisp-tender.

Serving size:

4 servings
