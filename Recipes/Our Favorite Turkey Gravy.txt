Our Favorite Turkey Gravy

6 cups broth, divided
Neck, giblets and other trimmings
1 onion, chopped
1 carrot, chopped
1 celery rib, chopped
8 springs parsley
2 springs thyme
2 garlic cloves, peeled
1/2 t pepper
1/4 t salt
1/3 white wine
4 T butter
5 T flour
1/4 drippings

Bring 2 cups and turkey to boil, cook until evaporates (20min) and found forms, about 2-4 minutes longer.

Add onions, celery, parsley, thyme, garlic, pepper, and salt. Cook until onion is translucent, 8-10 minutes.

Add wine, scrap up bits, then add 4 cups broth. Simmer for 1 hour then strain.

Melt butter and add flour. Cook until golden brown, 5-8 minutes. Whisk in stock and simmer 5 minutes. Add drippings
