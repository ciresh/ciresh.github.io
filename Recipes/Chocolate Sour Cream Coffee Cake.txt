Chocolate Source Cream Coffee Cake

1 Package Betty Crocker Devils Food Chocolate Cake Mix
4 Eggs
1 Package Instant Chocolate Pudding Mix
1 1/2 Cups Sour Cream 
1/2 Cup Warm Water
Small Package of Semi-sweet chocolate chips
3/4 Vegetable Oil

Mix all ingredients except chocolate chips then fold in chocolate chips.

Bake at 350 for 50 minutes in a greased and floured tube pan
