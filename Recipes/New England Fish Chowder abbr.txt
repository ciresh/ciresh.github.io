New England Fish Chowder
	2 tablespoons unsalted butter (1.5)
Melt in pan
	2 onions, cut into 1/2-inch dice (1.5)
	4 ounces salt pork, rind removed, rinsed, and cut into 2 pieces (3)
	1 1/2 teaspoons minced fresh thyme (1)
	3/4 salt (1/2)
	1 bay leaf
Cook, stirring occasionally, until onions are softened but not browned, 3 to 5 minutes	
	5 cups water (3.75)
Add, bring to Simmer then remove from heat
	2 pounds skinless cod fillets , sliced crosswise into 6 equal pieces (1.5)
Add to pan, let stand 5 minutes until opaque and near cook. Transfer fish to bowl.
	1 1/2 pounds Yukon Gold potatoes , peeled and cut into 1/2-inch dice (1)
Add to pan, medium-high heat to simmer, cook until tender 20 minutes
	2 cups whole milk (1.5, 2/3+ half & half, rest 1 percent)
	1 tablespoon cornstarch (2 1/4 teaspoons)
	1/2 teaspoon pepper 
Whisk, add to pan, bring to simmer. Add fish & juices, remove from heat, cover let stand for 5 minutes
Remove port, bay leaf, break up fish to smaller pieces then serve.
