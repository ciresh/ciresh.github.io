Green Bean Casserole Double Batch
2 can (10 3/4 oz.) Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 cup milk
2 tsp. soy sauce
1/4 tsp. Pepper
2 16 oz. frozen french style green beans
2 2/3 cups french fried onions

Cook green beans 5-8 minutes in boiling water.
Heat soup and milk together. Mix soup, milk, soy, pepper, beans and 1 1/3 cup onions in 3-qt. casserole.
BAKE at 350°F. for 25 min. or until hot.
STIR and then sprinkle with remaining onions. Bake 5 min.


Delmonico Potatoes
source: Anne Duross (Grammy)

1 1/2 cups cubed boiled potatoes
1 cup grated milk cheese
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons flour
1/8 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp celery salt
1 cup milk
1 tablespoon melted butter or margarine
1/2 cup dried bread crumps

Put half of the cooked potatoes into greased 1 1/2 qt casserole. Melt butter in saucepan, add flour which has been mixed with salt, pepper and celery salt. Gradually add milk. Stirring constantly bring to boiling and allow to boil 2 minutes stirring constantly. Combine 1 tablespoon melted butter and bread crumbs and toss well. Top potatoes with 1/2 cheese buttered bread crumbs and cream sauce. Add remaining potatoes and soon. Bake in 400 degree oven 10-14 minutes until golden.


Notes: Make 8 cu potatoes, 4 cu white sauce, 2 cup cheese. Needed more cheese.
